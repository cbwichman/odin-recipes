Burgers

Description

Burgers are a great cheat day meal. I'm not a cheese fan, so I usually go with a hamburger.

Ingredients

  • Burger Patties
  • Buns
  • Condiments
  • Your favorite toppings like pickles, tomatoes, lettuce, etc.

Steps

  1. Preheat the grill to the appropriate temperature
  2. Cook the burgers to your desired doneness
  3. Toast the buns if you prefer
  4. Prepare and add all condiments and toppings
